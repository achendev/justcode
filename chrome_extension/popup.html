    Permissions Required

    JustCode needs clipboard access to function correctly. Please enable it in the extension's settings.

    How to enable:
    1. Click the "Go to Settings" button below.
    2. The extension's details page will open in a new tab.
    3. Click on "Site settings" or "Permissions".
    4. Find "Clipboard" in the permissions list.
    5. Change its value from "Ask" or "Block" to "Allow".
    6. Return to this popup and click "Reload".
